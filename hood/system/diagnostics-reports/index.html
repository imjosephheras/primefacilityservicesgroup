Frequently Asked Questions

How often should hood systems be diagnosed?

+
Professional diagnostics should be performed annually at minimum, and more frequently for high-volume kitchens or systems with history of issues. We recommend quarterly or semi-annual diagnostics as part of a comprehensive maintenance program.

What makes your reports NFPA 96 compliant?

+
All our technicians hold current NFPA 96 certifications and our reports include complete compliance verification, component assessment, and recommendations aligned with all applicable standards and regulations.

How long does a diagnostic take?

+
A comprehensive diagnostic typically takes 2-4 hours depending on system complexity. We work around your schedule to minimize operational disruption, and can often accommodate off-hours inspections.

Can you provide emergency diagnostics?

+
Yes, we offer 24/7 emergency diagnostic services. Contact us immediately if you have concerns about your hood system's operation, and our certified technicians will provide rapid assessment and recommendations.
