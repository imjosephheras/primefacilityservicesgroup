Our Journey

From Humble Beginnings to Industry Leaders

What started as a small Houston-based janitorial firm has grown into Texas's premier facility management company. Our journey began with a simple mission: deliver exceptional service that exceeds expectations.

Today, Prime serves six major Texas cities - Houston, Austin, Dallas, Fort Worth, San Antonio, and Corpus Christi - providing comprehensive facility solutions to hotels, restaurants, corporate offices, and event venues.

Our growth is built on the foundation of shared values with our clients: quality, consistency, and steadfastness. We handle the details so you can focus on what matters most - your business and your guests.
