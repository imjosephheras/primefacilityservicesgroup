Prime Clock App  ·  Effective as of publication date

Prime Clock App is designed to help employees clock in and out of their work shifts. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect the information gathered through the app.

The app may access the following information:

Location data — to verify job site attendance
Camera — to capture photos for clock-in verification

This information is used strictly for attendance tracking and verification purposes.

How We Use Information

To ensure accurate time tracking
To prevent misuse of the system

We do not sell or share personal data with third parties. Your information stays within Prime Facility Services Group's systems.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

User Consent

By using the app, users consent to the collection and use of information as described in this Privacy Policy.
