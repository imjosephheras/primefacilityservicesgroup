Hood Cleaning Services

Complete Exhaust
System Coverage

Every component of your commercial kitchen exhaust system — cleaned, inspected, and documented to NFPA 96 standards.

Hood Canopy Cleaning
Full interior and exterior degreasing of hood canopy surfaces, baffles, and grease collection troughs.
Grease Filter Service
Removal, deep cleaning, and reinstallation of all baffle and mesh grease filters to peak efficiency.
Duct System Cleaning
Complete grease duct cleaning from hood collar to exhaust termination. Access panels installed as needed.
Exhaust Fan Cleaning
Rooftop exhaust fan degreasing, belt inspection, and performance verification with service report.
Fire Suppression Inspection
Visual inspection and documentation of suppression nozzles, fusible links, and ansul system integrity.
Compliance Documentation
Certified before/after inspection reports, service certificates, and NFPA 96 sticker placement.
Scheduled Maintenance
Quarterly, semi-annual, and annual service programs calibrated to your kitchen's grease load and NFPA 96 frequency requirements.
Emergency Service
24/7 emergency response for pre-inspection failures or immediate fire marshal compliance requirements.
