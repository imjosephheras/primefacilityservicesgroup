## Getting Started

### Accessing the Application

1. **Open your web browser** (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge recommended)
2. **Navigate to the application URL**
3. **Enter the password** when prompted
   - Contact your administrator for the password
   - The session remains active for 24 hours

### Main Dashboard

After logging in, you'll see three calculator options:

- **Kitchen Calculator** - For general kitchen cleaning quotes
- **Vent Hood Calculator** - For specialized vent hood cleaning
- **Timesheet Calculator** - For tracking labor costs and hours

## Kitchen Calculator

### Overview
The Kitchen Calculator helps you create professional quotes for commercial kitchen cleaning services with automatic profit optimization.

### Basic Operation

#### Step 1: Choose Service Type
- **Internal Service**: Using your own employees
- **Subcontractor**: Using external contractors

#### Step 2: Enter Basic Information
1. **Number of Workers** - How many people will work on this job
2. **Hours per Day** - Hours each worker will work
3. **Number of Days** - Total days for the job
4. **Materials Cost** - Daily cost of cleaning supplies
5. **Equipment Cost** - Daily cost of equipment usage

#### Step 3: Additional Options
- **Holiday Service** - Check if service is on a holiday (50% surcharge)
- **Outside Houston** - Check if job is outside Houston area
- **Include Insurance** - Include workers' comp and liability insurance

### Advanced Features

#### Configuration Tab
Access advanced settings:
- **Pay Rates** - Set hourly rates for workers and supervisors
- **Transport Costs** - Configure transportation fees
- **Tax Rates** - Adjust payroll tax and insurance rates
- **Target Profit** - Set your desired profit margin

#### Operational Costs
Expand the "Operational Costs Breakdown" to see:
- Regular supplies (6%)
- Additional equipment (2.75%)
- Uniforms & safety gear (2.5%)
- Communications (1%)
- Administrative overhead (5%)

#### Profit Options
1. **Standard Markup** - Automatic markup based on job duration
2. **Custom Markup** - Set your own markup percentage
3. **Target Grand Total** - Enter desired total, system calculates markup

#### Commission Settings
- Set sales commission percentage
- Enable commission splitting between multiple salespeople
- View net profit after commissions

### Generating Quotes

#### PDF Quote Generation
1. Click **"Generate PDF Quote"**
2. Fill in client information:
   - Business name
   - Contact person
   - Email and phone
   - Service address
   - Quote validity period
3. Click **"Generate PDF"**
4. PDF automatically downloads to your computer

#### Work Order Creation
1. Click **"Create Work Order"**
2. Enter client details
3. Add service descriptions:
   - Service title
   - Detailed scope of work
   - Special instructions
4. Click **"Generate Work Order PDF"**

### Understanding the Results

#### Cost Breakdown
- **Labor Cost**: Base wages for all workers
- **Payroll Tax**: Employment taxes (17%)
- **Workers' Comp**: Insurance based on rate (1.88%)
- **Transport**: Daily transportation costs
- **Materials**: Cleaning supplies
- **Equipment**: Equipment usage fees

#### Pricing Summary
- **Subtotal**: All costs before markup
- **Markup**: Profit margin added
- **Holiday Surcharge**: 50% extra for holiday service
- **Total Price**: Final price before rounding
- **Grand Total**: Final rounded price

#### Profit Analysis
- **Net Profit**: Profit after all costs
- **Cost %**: Percentage of price that goes to costs
- **Profit %**: Your profit margin
- **Company Profit**: Profit after sales commissions

## Vent Hood Calculator

### Overview
Specialized calculator for commercial kitchen exhaust hood cleaning services.

### Key Differences from Kitchen Calculator

#### Hood-Specific Inputs
- **Number of Hoods**: Total hoods to clean
- **Large Hoods**: Count of large hoods ($650 each)
- **Small Hoods**: Count of small hoods ($550 each)
- **Cleaning Frequency**: Monthly, quarterly, or annual

#### Pricing Structure
- Uses per-hood pricing instead of hourly rates
- Automatic price calculation based on hood size
- Frequency affects total annual cost

### Using the Calculator

1. **Enter Hood Information**
   - Total number of hoods
   - Breakdown by size (large/small)
   - Select cleaning frequency

2. **Choose Service Method**
   - Internal crew
   - Subcontractor (enter per-hood cost)

3. **Review Pricing**
   - See individual hood costs
   - View frequency-based pricing
   - Check annual contract value

4. **Generate Documents**
   - Create professional quotes
   - Generate work orders
   - Include safety compliance notes

## Timesheet Calculator

### Overview
Track employee hours and calculate comprehensive labor costs including all taxes and overhead.

### Setting Up Positions

#### Adding Workers
1. Click **"Add Position"**
2. Enter position name (e.g., "Cleaner", "Supervisor")
3. Set quantity (number of workers in this position)
4. Enter hourly rate
5. Input hours for each day of the week

#### Managing Positions
- **Edit**: Click on any field to modify
- **Delete**: Click trash icon (minimum one position required)
- **Duplicate**: Add similar positions quickly

### Entering Hours

#### Daily Hours
- Enter hours worked for each day
- Use decimal format (8.5 for 8 hours 30 minutes)
- Tab between fields for quick entry
- Total hours update automatically

#### Week Overview
- See daily totals at bottom
- Grand total shows total hours for all positions
- Visual indicators for overtime days (>8 hours)

### Cost Analysis

#### Understanding Costs
The calculator shows comprehensive employment costs:

1. **Base Labor Cost**
   - Weekly: Direct wages
   - Monthly: Weekly × 4.33

2. **Payroll Taxes** (Texas rates)
   - Social Security: 6.2%
   - Medicare: 1.45%
   - FUTA: 0.6%
   - SUTA: 2.7%

3. **Insurance & Benefits**
   - Workers' Compensation: 2.5%
   - General Liability: $150/month
   - Uniforms: $50/month

4. **Overhead**
   - Supplies: $200/month
   - Administration: 5%

#### Markup & Pricing
- Adjust markup percentage with slider
- See selling price with profit margin
- View commission calculations
- Analyze final profit

### Visualizations

#### Charts
Two interactive charts help visualize data:

1. **Hours Distribution**
   - Bar chart showing daily hours
   - Stacked by position
   - Hover for details

2. **Cost Breakdown**
   - Pie chart of all costs
   - See percentage of each component
   - Understand where money goes

### Exporting Data

#### Export Options
1. **Print/PDF**: Generate formatted report
2. **CSV Export**: Download for Excel
3. **Screenshot**: Capture current view

## Tips & Best Practices

### Accurate Quotes

1. **Always verify inputs** before generating quotes
2. **Include all costs** - don't forget materials and equipment
3. **Consider location** - use "Outside Houston" for distant jobs
4. **Plan for holidays** - 50% surcharge is automatic

### Efficiency Tips

1. **Save templates** - Use browser bookmarks for common configurations
2. **Use keyboard shortcuts** - Faster than clicking
3. **Batch similar quotes** - Change only what differs
4. **Review before sending** - Check PDF before emailing

### Professional Presentation

1. **Complete client info** - All fields create professional impression
2. **Add notes** - Explain special requirements
3. **Set validity dates** - Protect against price changes
4. **Include all services** - Detailed work orders prevent disputes

### Data Management

1. **Regular backups** - Export important quotes
2. **Clear old data** - Reset calculator for fresh start
3. **Document changes** - Note configuration adjustments
4. **Train new users** - Share this manual

### Optimization Strategies

1. **Understand markup** - Know how days affect pricing
2. **Use profit targets** - Let system calculate optimal markup
3. **Monitor commissions** - Factor into final pricing
4. **Review regularly** - Adjust rates as costs change
